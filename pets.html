Emily's House of Cats

Van is named after the artist Vincent Van Gogh. This is because he is missing an ear. Van ia hyper and constantly curious. He's often gets into trouble and is in constant play mode.

Momo is a big scaredy cat with a big heart. He tends to be skidish and jumps at sudden movements. However, when all is quiet, he leaps into action to demand attention.

Sandy is a bully. Whether demanding pets or bothering another cat she always exudes an air of annouance.

Bailey is a sweetheart. She is gentle and at the old age of 14 is aging gracefully. She enjoys sleeping with her sister, Buster.

Buster is 14 years old and doesn't show any signs of slowing down. She is just as lively and active as the day we got her. Although she can be a bother at times, she's a great cuddler.

Butter is a grumpy old lady. She can often be found hiding away in her favorite sleeping spot. Despite her grumpiness, when she wants to she can be a sweetheart.
